A guide to develop your own Mozaïk extension

So that’s it, you installed the Mozaïk demo, installed and configured some extensions but you want your own because you didn’t found the one which interact with the brand new service you’re just using.

Don’t be shy, take a deep breath and carefully follow this guide, you should be able to publish your crazy extension in a day or two (depending on your knowledge of the underlying technologies of course).
